Game Instructions

  • Objective: Avoid pipes and stay in the air as long as possible!
  • How to Play: Press Spacebar to make the bird flap and move upwards.
  • Gravity: The bird falls slowly due to gravity, so use spacebar to control it.
  • Game Over: The game ends when the bird hits the ground, top, or pipes.
  • Restart: Press Spacebar again after game over to restart the game.
